Darkwinds game guide

Welcome to the starting place to become a great pirate. Please note this documentation is a work in progress.

Getting Started

Requirements

To play darkwinds you need:

  • A Desktop computer with Chrome, Firefox, or Brave
  • The Metamask extension installed

Installing Metamask

Go to metamask.io and follow the instructions.

Obtaining Cards

To start a Darkwinds match, you need at least 10 cards. the quickest way to obtain them is to purchase a Booster Pack that contains 10, 25 or 50 random cards. To do so, you will need an Ethereum wallet, like Metamask.

Building your Deck

To build your deck, you must have at least 10 cards. (See Obtaining Cards)

Match Objective

The objective of the match is to take over the enemy's ship. A player is taken over when the health counter drops to zero.
